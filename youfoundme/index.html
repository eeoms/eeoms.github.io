Riddle

In realms of dreams, where shadows play,
To me, they come, a special bouquet.
Not roses red, nor lilies fair,
But "eoms" rare, beyond compare.

A poppy enchanted, whispers sweet,
Its petals dance to a mystic beat.
To find this gift, a quest begun,
In dreams untold, where moon meets sun.

What am I, the treasure sought?
Deliver "eoms," with love, be brought.
A bloom of magic, a whispered plea,
Solve this riddle, and set it free.
